Ground Turkey Mix

Description

This Ground Turkey and Rice mix is spicy and savory to the max.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs Ground Turkey
  • 3 Anaheim or 2 Sweet Peppers
  • 2 Serrano Peppers
  • 1 Medium Onion
  • 2 Cups Rice
  • Avocado Oil
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Lemon
  • Cilantro

Steps

  1. Begin cooking the rice in a pot or rice cooker
  2. Dice the onion and peppers
  3. In a hot pan, add avocado oil and the diced onion. Mix occasionally
  4. After the onions have developed some color, Add in the peppers and ground turkey
  5. Add salt and pepper to taste
  6. Mix occasionally, waiting for 3-5 minutes between to allow for browning
  7. After rice is done, both elements should be ready
  8. Season with additional salt, pepper, lemon, and cilantro
