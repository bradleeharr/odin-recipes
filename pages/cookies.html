Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

These Cookies are the best simple cookies. They have a good amount of salt, darkness, and butteryness results in a cookie that I believe is better than any other for the time it takes to make!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Sugar
  • 1 tbsp Molasses
  • 1 cup (2 sticks) Butter
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Baking Soda
  • 1 tsp Vanilla
  • 1 Egg
  • ~1 cup Flour
  • Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. Melt the butter in the microwave
  2. Mix sugar, molasess, butter, salt, baking soda, and vanilla
  3. Add in egg and 1 cup flour, Add more flour if dough is thin
  4. Add in Chocolate Chips
  5. Chill the dough if needed
  6. Bake at 350F for 12 minutes
